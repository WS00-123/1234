Company Profile

Founded in London in 1962 by Jim Marshall, Marshall Amplification is a world-renowned British audio manufacturer focusing on the R&D, production and sales of guitar amplifiers, speaker cabinets and related audio accessories. For over 60 years, Marshall has created iconic British rock tone and become the preferred audio brand for countless professional musicians worldwide.

Our factory has complete production lines covering solid-state amplifiers, tube amplifiers and portable practice equipment. We have independent acoustic laboratories to optimize tone circuits, speaker units and cabinet structures. The Marshall G11 11W practice amp is our star entry-level product, designed for students and amateur guitar lovers.

We maintain global sales networks covering Europe, North America, Southeast Asia and China, cooperating with thousands of musical instrument dealers to deliver high-quality audio equipment to every music enthusiast.

Our Products

Marshall divides products into three core lines: practice amplifiers, performance amplifiers and professional tube amplifiers. The Marshall G11 belongs to the MG desktop practice series, the most cost-effective choice for daily indoor training.

  • Marshall G11 11W Solid-State Amp: 8-inch custom speaker, independent Gain, Volume, Treble and Bass knobs, equipped with AUX input and headphone silent output, compact and portable.
  • MG Series Mid-range Models: 15W/30W amplifiers with optional Bluetooth function, suitable for small indoor band performances.
  • DSL Professional Tube Series: Multi-channel vacuum tube amplifiers for stage live, recording studio and professional performers.
  • Accessories: Instrument cables, foot switches, speaker cabinets and custom protective covers matching all amplifiers.

All products pass strict acoustic and safety tests before leaving the factory to ensure stable tone performance and long service life.

After-sales Service

Marshall provides standardized global after-sales system to solve equipment faults and usage problems for all customers.

  • Two-year official warranty: All new amplifiers enjoy free maintenance for non-artificial damage within 2 years from purchase date.
  • Global authorized maintenance centers: Professional technicians receive unified brand training to repair circuit, speaker and control knob faults.
  • Online technical support: Customers can submit questions via official email to obtain tone adjustment guidance and fault diagnosis solutions.
  • Spare parts supply: Original knobs, power adapters and speaker units are available for long-term replacement.
  • Return & exchange policy: Unused products with complete packaging support return within 7 days under standard conditions.

Our service team responds to customer feedback within 24 working hours to minimize the impact of equipment failure on practice and performance.
